Simulation of a Bacteria Colony



A modelling project modelling the population of a bacteria colony on a 2D surface



PROJECT TITLE : Modelling the population of a bacteria colony



Overview : We simulate bacteria in different colonies which are in either synergistic or competetive relation with one another in a fixed environement on two types of surfaces. We wish to deduce the ideal combination of surfaces to control the size of the population.
